Press Events

FAZ3A flourished on the 22nd of October 2023 when we held our first event, raising up to 1600BD and hosting 230 spectators. This success was one of first in the island and has since inspired many other non profits to throw fundraising events in the exhilarating spirit of football!



We held the first glow in the dark padel event in Bahrain on the 23rd of February 2024, and it was an astounding success! Thank you to everyone who participated.



FAZ3A flourished on the 15th of November 2024 at its 2nd successful Charity Football Match. We attracted over 400 spectators from across the island and 120 players! Congratulations to Mix Grills and The Eagles for winning the tournament.
